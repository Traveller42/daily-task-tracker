Created by Daniel Marino. Follow the Daily Task Tracker on Github.

  • Each bubble represents 15 minutes.
  • Click "Add Task" or press SHIFT + ENTER to create a new task.
  • Enter a task description, and check a bubble for the amount of time you spent on it.
  • Your tasks and time spent are automatically saved using local storage.
  • Individual tasks can be removed by clicking the "×" to the left of the task.
  • Click "Download CSV" to download a CSV file of your current tasks.
  • Click "Clear All Tasks" to erase all tasks (this will also erase local storage).
  • The Daily Task Tracker is inspired by Dave Seah's Emergent Task Timer.
